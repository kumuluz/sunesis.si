Data Security

12. To ensure the security and privacy of your personal data, we have implemented various technical measures in our backend systems. When you create an account, your email address and password are securely transmitted to our servers using industry-standard encryption protocols, such as SSL/TLS. Your password is then hashed using a strong, one-way cryptographic function, such as bcrypt, with a unique salt for each password. This means that your actual password is never stored in our database, and even if our systems were compromised, attackers would not be able to retrieve your original password.

13. All personal data, including your name, vehicle details, and ride history, are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption, which is a highly secure encryption standard. Access to this data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job functions, such as providing customer support or improving our services. We maintain detailed access logs and regularly monitor them for any suspicious activity.

14. When you use the app's features, such as live ride tracking, your location data is transmitted to our servers using secure, encrypted connections. We use advanced anonymization techniques, such as tokenization, to replace sensitive data with a non-sensitive equivalent, called a token, that has no extrinsic or exploitable meaning or value. This helps to protect your privacy and reduce the risk of your personal data being compromised.

15. We also employ pseudonymization techniques to replace personally identifiable information with a pseudonym, such as a unique identifier, in our backend systems. This means that your personal data is processed in such a manner that it can no longer be attributed to you without the use of additional information, which is kept separately and subject to technical and organizational measures to ensure that your personal data is not attributed to an identified or identifiable natural person.

16. Our backend systems are regularly tested for vulnerabilities and are updated with the latest security patches to protect against potential threats. We also conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to identify and address any weaknesses in our systems.
