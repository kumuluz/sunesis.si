Purpose of Data Collection

By using the "GREEN.DAT.AI" EV Crowdsensing app, you acknowledge and consent to the collection and processing of your personal data for the following purposes:

1. Login and Registration: We collect your email address, password, first name, and last name to create and maintain your account, enable secure access to the app, communicate with you, and assist with account recovery processes if needed.

2. Vehicle Details: We collect information about your vehicle, including the Bluetooth name, brand, model, variant, battery capacity, and specified consumption, to provide accurate energy consumption estimates and optimize charging recommendations based on your specific vehicle's characteristics.

3. Ride History and Trip Information: We collect data about your trips, including trip names, vehicle types, completion status, dates, times, vehicle information, and route details, to track your trip history, analyze your driving patterns, and improve your overall user experience.

4. Geolocation Settings: We collect your preferences for geolocation settings, such as desired accuracy, distance filter, location update interval, and fastest location update interval, to allow you to customize the app's location tracking features according to your needs and preferences.

5. Live Ride Tracking: We collect your real-time location data, including coordinates, speed, altitude, and distance traveled, during live ride tracking to provide you with accurate navigation, real-time updates, and personalized recommendations.
